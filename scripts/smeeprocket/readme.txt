Transgender and Intersex mod created by SmeepRocket. (http://www.bay12forums.com/smf/index.php?topic=146312.0)
Transgender and Intersex mod ported to PyDwarf by Sophie Kirschner. (http://www.pineapplemachine.com)

This adds transmales, transfemales, intersex males, and intersex females to the dwarf fortress fortress mode game. This does not add it to adventure mode.
